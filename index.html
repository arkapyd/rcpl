Odds & Bets Explained

// how the predictive engine works

calculation: odds are dynamically generated by blending each team's manager performance index (50%), recent form momentum (20%), head-to-head record vs that specific opponent (15%, combining S1–S3 league history with live S5 meetings), current-season win percentage (5%), and historical baseline rating (10%), plus a net-run-rate boost. the two strengths form a ratio that maps to standard fractional odds.

betting rules: the odds display the ratio in favour of the stronger team.

  • favourite: lower risk. profit = stake ÷ odds value.
  • underdog: higher risk. profit = stake × odds value.
  • loss: if your chosen team loses, you lose your entire stake.

note: all odds and calculations are for predictive and entertainment purposes only.
