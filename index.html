Odds & Bets Explained

    
// how the predictive engine works

    
    

      
strength: manager index 50% · form 20% · head-to-head 15% (S1–S4 + live S5) · win% 5% · historical rating 10%, plus an NRR boost. the two teams' strengths form a ratio mapped to fractional odds.

      
payouts: favourite profit = stake ÷ odds · underdog profit = stake × odds · a loss forfeits the whole stake.

      
predictive & entertainment only.
