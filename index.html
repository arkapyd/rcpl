Franchise Legacies

Punjab Kings (PBKS)

The perennial bridesmaids of the RCPL. Despite finishing as runners-up in all three previous seasons, they are statistically the most dominant team in league history. With an absurd cumulative W/L ratio of 5.58 and a strike rate over 218, they are the undisputed kings of the group stage, desperately hunting for their elusive maiden title.

Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR)

1-time RCPL Champions. They have maintained an elite level of consistency across all three seasons. Boasting a stellar cumulative win/loss ratio of 3.91 and aggressive batting, they are a complete package aiming for back-to-back titles.

Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB)

1-time RCPL Champions. Known for their explosive batting firepower throughout the league's history, they consistently post massive numbers. They remain heavyweights with a fearsome top order.

Delhi Capitals (DC)

A resilient squad that hangs tough in the middle of the pack. Their historical strike rate shows promise, but they need to convert close games into victories to improve their sub-1.00 win ratio and finally grab a title.

Rajasthan Royals (RR)

The giant-killers who have historically struggled with consistency across all three seasons. They are looking to break out of the bottom half of the table and improve their overall win percentage.

Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH)

Facing an uphill battle historically, struggling with the ball and holding an economy rate over 10 across three cumulative seasons. They are looking for a complete structural turnaround this year.

Mumbai Indians (MI)

A franchise with a glorious past and 1 RCPL Championship - won in the inaugural season. However, their cumulative stats reflect a massive struggle since their title run, holding an overall W/L ratio of just 0.27. They are desperate to reclaim their throne and rebuild their legacy.

Chennai Super Kings (CSK)

It has been an incredibly difficult three seasons for the legendary yellow franchise, holding the lowest win rate (0.11) and strike rate (98.20) in cumulative RCPL history. They are in full rebuild mode and hungry for redemption.

Lucknow Super Giants (LSG)

A brand new entrant to the RCPL in Season 4. With a completely clean slate and zero baggage, they are the unpredictable wildcards ready to disrupt the established hierarchy.

Gujarat Titans (GT)

Making their highly anticipated debut in Season 4. They have everything to prove and are ready to make a massive splash to build a new legacy from the ground up.
